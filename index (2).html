The Technology

HOW SUNVECTOR WORKS

A smart, compact system that keeps your solar panel aimed perfectly at the sun from dawn to dusk — automatically.

1

Sense

Light-dependent resistors (LDR sensors) continuously detect the intensity and direction of incoming sunlight in real time.

2

Process

An Arduino/Raspberry Pi microcontroller computes the optimal panel angle based on sensor readings.

3

Track

Servo or DC motors rotate the panel mount to precisely follow the sun's arc across the sky throughout the day.

4

Monitor

A built-in IoT module lets you view real-time energy output and system performance on your device.
